The folders below contain the sources of version 4.1.0 of the cvodes integrator 
form the sundails suite. The sundails suite is distributed by Lawrence Livermore 
national laboratories.

All Makefiles and files related to Fortran have been left out.
Only the file include/sundails/sundails_version.h and cvodes/cvodes_impl.h 
have been slightly modified from its original to support __float128.
